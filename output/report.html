Synthesis

ICE Cotton No.2 futures present a nuanced picture where fundamental bearishness meets extreme positioning. The supply-demand balance clearly supports lower prices - global production exceeds consumption by 3.4M bales, stocks are at 5-year highs, and demand growth is anemic. This creates an asymmetric risk profile: downside is limited (shorts have already been built), while upside risk is significant (any positive catalyst could trigger violent short-covering).
